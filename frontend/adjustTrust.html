Scraper Trust Settings:

Increase trust if the year found is:



Use this field to reduce the overall trust by the inputed amount. This can be done to keep trust lower and reduce 'Trust inflation'. Default is 1.


Not between 0 and 100: Trust is increased from data where the date is not between 0 and 100.
For example: 564 and -1000 are increased, 50 and 21 are not.

Not a multiple of 5: Trust is increased from data where the date is not a multiple of 5.
For example: 564 and -1234 are increased, 1000 and -50 are not.

Not between 0 and 10: Trust is increased from data where the date is not between 0 and 10.
For example: 564 and -1234 are increased, 2 and 9 are not.

Different numbers: Trust is increased from data where the date is a combination of many different numbers.
For example: 564 and -1234 are increased, 1000 and 1776 are not.

Reduce trust: This field can be used to reduce the overall trust by the inputed amount.
