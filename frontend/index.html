Scraper











This scraper creates a Dataset of images and year numbers of artifacts by scraping a selection of websites specified by the user.

The dataset produced, is a list of images paired with their corrisponding year of creation. It contains a CSV file that maps each image to its year of creation and a trust score, which can be used to determine the accuracy of the year of creation. The trust score is calculated by taking into account the trust parameters given by the user and a form of proof-of-work where data with more information is seen as more likly to be correct.

Once scraped (Start), the dataset can be downloaded from the Display Data page.
